Welcome to HelpHub!

Feeding America

Feeding America is a nationwide network of food banks and charitable organizations dedicated to alleviating hunger in the United States. They work to provide nutritious food to millions of Americans facing food insecurity through a network of food pantries, soup kitchens, and shelters. By partnering with local communities, businesses, and individuals, Feeding America distributes billions of pounds of food each year, aiming to address both short-term hunger relief and long-term solutions to food insecurity.

United Way Worldwide

United Way Worldwide is a global nonprofit organization focused on improving communities and advancing social causes around the world. Through partnerships with local organizations, businesses, governments, and individuals, United Way works to address issues such as education, income stability, and health. They mobilize resources, advocate for policy changes, and implement programs aimed at creating lasting positive impacts in communities, empowering individuals to reach their full potential.

St. Jude Children's Research Hospital

St. Jude Children's Research Hospital is a leading pediatric treatment and research facility dedicated to treating and curing pediatric catastrophic diseases, particularly childhood cancer. Located in Memphis, Tennessee, St. Jude provides cutting-edge medical care to children regardless of their ability to pay. Their groundbreaking research discoveries are shared freely with medical institutions worldwide, contributing to the global fight against childhood diseases. Families never receive a bill from St. Jude for treatment, travel, housing, or food, allowing them to focus solely on their child's healing..

Goodwill

Goodwill Industries International is a nonprofit organization that provides job training, employment placement services, and other community-based programs for people facing barriers to employment. Through its network of local Goodwill organizations, it offers job training programs, career counseling, and support services to individuals with disabilities, those who lack education or job experience, and others facing challenges in the job market. Goodwill also operates thrift stores where donated goods are sold to fund their programs, promoting sustainability and community engagement.
